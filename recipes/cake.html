Back-of-the-Box Hershey Cake

Description

A classic and historical cake recipe from Hershey. This cake recipe was created post WW2 with Hershey chocolate bars, it has since appeared on as a back-of-box recipe for many hershey products including their coco powder.

Ingredients

Cake:

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspons baking soda
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Frosting:

  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, at room temp
  • 5 1/3 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven 350 F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans
  2. Make Cake: Stir sugar, flour, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, and vanilla, mix for 3 mins with electric mixe. Stir in boiling water by hand.
  4. Pour evenly into the perpared pans.
  5. Bake in oven for 30-35 mins. Let cool for 10 mins before removing from pans.
  6. Make Frosting: Cream butter with electric mixer until light a fluffy. Stir in confectioners' sugar and cocoa alternately with milk and vanilla. Beat to a smooth spreading consistency.
  7. Split the layers of the cooled cake horizontally, cover the top of each layer with frosting, then stack them onto a serving plate.
  8. Frost the outside of the cake with remaining frosting.
  9. Enjoy!
