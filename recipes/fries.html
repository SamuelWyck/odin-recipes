Crispy Seasoned French Fries

Description

These fries are perfect for a gourmet homemade meal to get you out of the takeout habit. Paired with a cheddar-bacon burger, and maybe even a piece of Hershey's chocolate cake, This recipe will keep your family coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 lbs russet potatoes, peeled
  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water, or as needed

Description

  1. Slice potatoes into French fries and place them into a large bowl of cold water.
  2. Heat oil in a heavy saucepan or large skillet to 350 degrees F.
  3. Sift flour, garlic salt, onion salt, paprika, and salt into a large bowl. Then gradually stir in enough water to make a slightly thick batter that can be drizzled from a spoon.
  4. Drain fries and pat to dry; dip into hte batter one at a time.
  5. Lower fries carefully into the hot oil in batches to prevent them from clumping together. Fry until golden brown and crispy on all sides, about 7 to 10 mins.
  6. Drain fries on a paper towel-lined plate.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
